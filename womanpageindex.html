About us

Feel Safe is web app which is used for women safety.The app has a login page in which you have to enter username and password.If you are using the app for first time then create an account.When a person is in emergency then the location of that person will be sent to tow of their contact and the location get stored in the database of the webpage.Similarly the app has three button click option where anyone of them police,ambulance or firestation can be contacted if the respective button is clicked.This app also contain the women help line.The registered users data will be stored in the webpage database.This webpage will be accessed by the employee and the new employ will be added by admin.Feel Safe webpage consist home,about,contact us,admin signin and employee login.
